Admissions How to apply

Families interested in applying to The Sultan’s School are required to complete and submit the application form, along with all the supporting documentation listed. Incomplete applications will not be accepted or processed.

Applicants living abroad are required to obtain an attestation from Ministry of Education confirming the year group they will be applying for. This document must be submitted with the application the admissions process can begin.

The full list of documentation you need to provide is listed below:

  • A completed application form
  • End of Year report, SY 2018-2019
  • Term One report, SY 2019-20
  • Term Two Report, SY 2019-20
  • End of Year report, SY 2019-20 (if issued)
  • Copy of the student’s birth certificate
  • Copy of the student’s identification card and passport
  • Copy of the parents’ identification cards and passports
  • A recent passport-sized digital photograph
  • A copy of the student’s medical record and vaccination card

Application may please be sent to admissions@arc.edu.ng

In order for the applications to be processed a fee of RO30 is payable for each application (this fee is non-refundable). Kindly ensure the transfer of RO 30 per application to account number 0302010073260013, The Sultans School, Bank Muscat to facilitate this. Please contact Mr. Ahmed Al Mahrezi in the Finance Department if you have any questions regarding the bank transfer. His email address is ahmeds@sultansschool.edu.om.
